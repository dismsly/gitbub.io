We Understand the Dismissal Dilemma.

The end-of-day bell shouldn't signal the start of chaos. We've spoken with hundreds of parents and educators, and the challenges are universal.

For Educators & Administrators

Juggling last-minute phone calls, deciphering handwritten notes, managing long car lines, and the constant pressure of ensuring every single student is safely accounted for.

For Parents

The uncertainty of whether the school received the change-of-plans email, the anxiety of a delayed pickup, and the desire for clear, direct communication about their child's departure.

Grounded in Research, Developed with Care.

A tool for school safety can't be rushed. That's why Dismisly is currently in a dedicated Research & Development (R&D) phase. We are actively collaborating with teachers, administrators, and parents to build features that solve real-world problems.

Your feedback is essential in shaping a tool that is not only powerful but also incredibly easy to use.
